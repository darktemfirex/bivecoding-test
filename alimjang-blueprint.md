# Daycare AI Notification Generator Blueprint (어린이집 AI 알림장 생성기)

## Overview
A specialized AI tool designed for daycare teachers to quickly generate warm, personalized daily notifications (Alimjang). By combining visual context from photos, child-specific keywords, and reference text, the AI crafts unique messages that feel personal and professional.

## Detailed Features
- **Smart Image Preview:** Upload up to 10 photos and see them in a grid.
- **AI Prompt Engineering:** Uses Child Name, Keywords, and Reference Text to guide the Gemini AI.
- **Batch Readiness:** Designed to quickly switch between children/situations.
- **Premium UI:** Uses a soft pastel palette (Ivory, Soft Blue, Warm Yellow) to create a trustworthy and friendly atmosphere.
- **Copy & Export:** One-click copy for easy pasting into official daycare apps.

## Design Specs
- **Background:** Soft Ivory with a subtle linen texture.
- **Cards:** White with 2rem rounded corners and deep, soft shadows.
- **Buttons:** Gradient buttons (Warm Yellow to Orange) for a playful look.
- **Typography:** Rounded sans-serif (Pretendard or similar).
